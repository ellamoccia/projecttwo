CHALLAH BREAD (Grandma's recipe)

Ingredients

1 T. yeast
1 1/4 c. warm water
2 eggs; slightly beaten
1/3 c. honey
1/4 c. oil
1 t. salt
5-6 c. flour



Challah was one of the first things that my grandma and I bonded over.

As a child, my favorite part was painting on the egg wash, and getting to rip a chunk of warm bread for myself when it was done.
